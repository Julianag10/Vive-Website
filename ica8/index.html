Fact

Over 60% of Latino & African American children do not know how to swim, increasing the drowning risk in our community.

Our Impact

5800 children & their families learned to swim. Every year ViVe teaches over 500 kids & their families how to swim through our After School & Learn to Swim programs.

Fact

Minority children lack the access & opportunity of outdoor nature activities as well as physical wellness. These are essential to the mental health, cognitive development & the physical, social & emotional well-being of children.

Our Impact

4900 children enrolled in After School and outdoor recreation programs. Movement, social emotional and wellness & Youth Workforce development.

Fact

30,000 newcomers arrived in Denver Colorado in 2023. Most of them from Venezuela without permit to work while they waited for their Immigration appointment.

Our Impact

15,000 newcomers assisted. VIVE has supported through resources such as Food bags, Telefones, Bus passes, Mental health resources, Children's school and has housed over 4500 people a total of 1000 leases.

Our Partners

Through partnerships with local organizations and donations secured through grants and private funding, ViVe Wellness promotes physical and mental health, leadership, and social well-being to children and adults across Denver and surrounding cities.

We offer our services to organizations. ViVe is able to continue to offer the programs to communities of need through donations, grants and organizations partnering with ViVe for their projects. Consider us for your future projects: • ViVe can create and organize your Community Events events such as community 5K walks/runs, obstacle courses, community bike rides, park use initiative, company wellness programs and more! • Zumba and Yoga demonstrations to promote event cohesive participation and bring communities together. • Physical activity classes to organizations, communities, schools, after school programs, businesses, etc. • Wellness education and mental health classes • Diabetes prevention classes
