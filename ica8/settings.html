Our Privacy Statement

1. What data we collect

  • Form information: When you sign up as a volunteer, we collect the details you provide (such as your name, email, and selected program).
  • Interactions with the site: We may save your preferences (like chosen program or language) so the site remembers them the next time you visit.

2. Why we collect it

We collect this data to make your experience smoother. For example:

  • Remembering your details so you don’t have to retype them every time.
  • Showing you relevant program information based on your interests.

We do not share your data with third parties. It is only used to improve your experience on this site.

3. How long data is stored and where

  • Your information is stored locally in your own browser using local storage.
  • This means the data never leaves your device or gets sent to our servers.
  • Data may remain saved across browser sessions (even if you close and reopen your browser) until you clear it or until it expires automatically after a set time.

4. What controls you have

  • You can choose whether to allow data collection by using the “Allow site to save my data” setting.
  • You can view or clear all stored data at any time from the Settings page.
  • You can also disable data saving completely if you prefer.
