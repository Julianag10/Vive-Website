Breaking Barriers to Health Inequities

Building Healthier Futures for Every Family

Delivering culturally relevant wellness programs that break down systemic barriers for underserved communities to achieve lasting health, equity, and resilience.

We envision a world where gender, race, ethnicity, disability, education, income, origin and sexual orientation are no longer predictors of life and health outcomes.

Our Mission

We foster social justice, health, and gender equity through culturally relevant physical, mental, and social wellness programs.

Fact

Over 60% of Latino & African American children do not know how to swim, increasing the drowning risk in our community.

Our Impact

5800 children & their families learned to swim. Every year ViVe teaches over 500 kids & their families how to swim through our After School & Learn to Swim programs.

Fact

Minority children lack the access & opportunity of outdoor nature activities as well as physical wellness. These are essential to the mental health, cognitive development & the physical, social & emotional well-being of children.

Our Impact

4900 children enrolled in After School and outdoor recreation programs. Movement, social emotional and wellness & Youth Workforce development.

Fact

30,000 newcomers arrived in Denver Colorado in 2023. Most of them from Venezuela without permit to work while they waited for their Immigration appointment.

Our Impact

15,000 newcomers assisted. VIVE has supported through resources such as Food bags, Telefones, Bus passes, Mental health resources, Children's school and has housed over 4500 people a total of 1000 leases.
